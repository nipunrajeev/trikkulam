Pre-primary section has a well designed children's park.It is provided with all facilities which are sufficient for a child to study and enjoy.

Hi-Tech Rooms

All the class rooms are well furnished and provided with laptop,multimedia projector, sound system and internet connectivity.cctv surveilence is available in all building.

Science Lab

Science lab is one of the best in district.Large number of specimens,charts are available.Students have the opportunity to do simple experiments and observe different types of specimen.An electron microscope is available.

School has an auditorium.This auditorium is provided with nearly 200 seats and well designed Sound system.For conducting students programme this is more than enough.

Pre-primary section is working in the school since last five years.Class rooms are well designed to meet the needs of Lkg and Ukg students.They are getting homely atmosphere.
